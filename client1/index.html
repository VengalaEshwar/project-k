About NeuroPredict

Our endeavor employs cutting-edge algorithms like convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and decision trees to pioneer neurological disease prediction. By integrating various data streams such as brain scans, genetic markers, and medical histories, our goal is to create an innovative tool for early detection of conditions like Alzheimer's and Parkinson's. This proactive approach aims to facilitate timely interventions, improving patient outcomes and enabling personalized treatment strategies.
